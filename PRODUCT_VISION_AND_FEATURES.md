## Platform features and visions

### Feature ideas (grouped by area)
- **Core AI research**
  - Perplexity+OpenAI hybrid: live-sourced facts with structured outputs
  - Explain like I’m 5 / Pro mode toggles
  - Source spans/citations inline with confidence scores
- **Data & coverage**
  - Multi-asset support: mutual funds, ETFs, indices, commodities, crypto
  - Fundamentals deep-dive: segment/geography breakdowns, management quality scoring
  - Alt‑data hooks: app reviews, job postings, web traffic, ESG
- **Portfolio**
  - Auto-sync with brokers/CSV rules engine
  - Tax-aware P&L and realized/unrealized views
  - Factor exposure & risk: beta, sector, style tilts, VaR
  - What‑if rebalancing and goal-based planning
- **Charts & visualization**
  - Natural‑language charts (Charts 2) with multi-series compare
  - Templates: valuation comps, revenue bridges, cohort charts
  - Embeddable iframes with signed URLs
- **Screener & comps**
  - AI screener: “find capital‑efficient Indian SaaS < 30x EV/EBITDA”
  - Peer-set auto-detect and relative valuation
- **Signals & alerts**
  - Event-driven alerts: earnings, filings, broker downgrades
  - Signal library: golden cross, gap ups, momentum/mean reversion
- **Recommendations**
  - Structured thesis: bull/bear cases, catalysts, risks, sanity checks
  - Confidence calibration with historical backfill
- **Backtesting & paper trading**
  - Rule composer + walk-forward optimization
  - Paper trade with slippage and transaction costs
- **Collaboration**
  - Shared notes with permalinks, versioned reports
  - Comment on data points (inline annotations)
- **Personalization**
  - Investor profiles: risk, horizon, sectors; adaptive feed
  - Learning plan: quizzes on holdings, concept drills
- **Integrations**
  - Brokers (read-only first), Notion/Slack/Discord, Google Sheets
- **Compliance & trust**
  - Audit log of AI calls, data lineage
  - Country-specific disclaimers and suitability checks
- **Monetization**
  - Usage-based credits, Pro tier, Team seats, Data add-ons

### Roadmap sketch
- **Near-term (2–4 weeks)**
  - Expand Charts 2: multi-series, compare tickers, downloadable PNG/CSV
  - AI Screener MVP with promptable filters
  - Alerts for earnings + price % moves
  - Portfolio factor/risk snapshot
- **Mid-term (1–3 months)**
  - Broker/Sheets sync, rebalancer, goal planning
  - Structured recommendations v2 with calibration dashboard
  - Backtesting MVP + paper trading
  - Collaboration (shareable reports, notes)
- **Longer-term (3–9 months)**
  - Alt-data pipelines, enterprise admin, permissions
  - Social discovery (public research boards)
  - White-label and API monetization

### Distinct product visions
- **Consumer investing copilot**
  - Focus: simplicity, NL queries, alerts, auto-sync portfolios
  - Monetization: freemium + Pro
- **Pro research workstation**
  - Focus: deep data, screeners, backtests, exports, compliance trail
  - Monetization: monthly seat pricing
- **Advisor/wealth platform**
  - Focus: client portfolios, model allocations, auditability, reports
  - Monetization: per-seat + AUM-based tiers
- **Social/investor network**
  - Focus: shared theses, leaderboards, curated lists
  - Monetization: creator tools, premium communities
- **API/data platform**
  - Focus: chart/insight generation API, embeddings, report-as-a-service
  - Monetization: usage-based billing
- **Education-first**
  - Focus: interactive lessons tied to user holdings
  - Monetization: course bundles, certifications
- **Enterprise IR/competitive intel**
  - Focus: sector dashboards, alerts, workflow integrations (Slack/Notion)
  - Monetization: enterprise contracts
